About GeoFeeds

GeoFeeds is a professional GIS & satellite intelligence platform designed for South Africa with global analytical capabilities. The platform brings together high-resolution satellite data, spatial analytics and real-time environmental insights in a simple, modern interface accessible to everyone—from government planners to agricultural experts and real estate analysts.

Our Mission

To democratise geospatial intelligence by making professional mapping, satellite imagery and environmental analytics accessible and affordable for individuals, businesses and institutions across Africa.

Our Vision

To grow GeoFeeds into Africa’s most trusted geospatial intelligence network—empowering land management, infrastructure planning, agricultural sustainability, and data-driven decision-making at every level.

Founded by Xoli Xaba

With 22 years of experience as a GIS Specialist and Senior GIS Technician in the South African municipal sector, Xoli brings deep expertise in spatial planning, property valuation rolls, land use management, environmental assessment, wetland monitoring and geospatial data infrastructure.

Throughout his career, Xoli has worked extensively in land audits, municipal environmental studies, infrastructure networks, cadastral mapping and technical GIS support for government initiatives. GeoFeeds continues that tradition by using modern online tools to make high-level GIS intelligence accessible to a wider audience.
